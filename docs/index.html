Key features

4-step workflow

1. Pin the toolbar icon after installing.
2. Open any page and click “Draw On Screen”.
3. Draw, highlight, and add notes as needed.
4. Take a screenshot or copy it to clipboard.

Who is it for?

Students, teachers, designers, devs, managers—anyone who needs quick visual feedback on live webpages.
